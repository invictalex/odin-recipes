Souvlaki

Description

Souvlaki is a dish origninating in Greece and Cyprus. It involves chargrilling pieces of meat that have been marinaded using fresh ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 500 grams pork shoulder, diced
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp pepper
  • 1 lemon, juiced
  • 1 tbsp fresh coriander, finely chopped

Steps

  1. Light your coals, and wait for them to go white
  2. Meanwhile, toss the meat with the salt, pepper, and oregano
  3. Add in the olive oil and lemon juice, massaging it together. Let it sit in the fridge for 1-4 hours
  4. Skewer the meat cubes. Put them on the grill, turning regurlarly until they are cooked through.
